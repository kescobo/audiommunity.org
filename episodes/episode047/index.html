Episode 47 - If you're a hammer

Published: October 23, 2025
Duration: 0:59:58
Topics:
inflammationcell biologyinnate immunityTLR7SLERNAautoimmunity

Against all expectations, there's RNA attached to the surface of cells. Why? Who knows! But it almost certainly has implications for the immune system. As Matt and Kevin discuss in this episode, it turns out that cells add sugars to these RNAs (glycosylation) to hide them from the innate immune system, especially during apoptosis.
