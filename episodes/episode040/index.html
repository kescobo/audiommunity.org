Episode 40 - Mohawk Mice, Precious

In this episode, Matt and Kevin discuss an excellent paper showing how skin-regulatory Tregs have a distinct role in protecting hair follicles from autoreactive CD8 T-cells; it's a really clear illustration of why animal research is critical to advancing our understanding.
