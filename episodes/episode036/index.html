Episode 36 - Mice touching grass

Published: March 18, 2025
Duration: 2:07:06
Topics:
B-cellsantibodieswild micescience policy

You can now find and subscribe to us on youtube!

In this episode, we discuss a new paper showing how "rewilding" mice - basically letting them run around in a field - alters their immune system. Basically, it changes their B-cells.

Plus, we talk with Jeffrey Morgan, one of the organizers of the Atlanta edition of Stand Up For Science.
