Things are bleak in American academia here in 2025. Here, Matt and Kevin speak with Abbie Smith, an assistant professor at Emory's Hope Clinic, where she researches HIV and oversees clinical trials assessing the prevention and treatment of people with HIV. As you might imagine, the current regime isn't super happy about her work.

No paper in this episode, just some honest kvetching and an inspiring speech from Abbie about what we can aspire to.
